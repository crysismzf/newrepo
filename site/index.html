Life is puzzles

Life is puzzles ... from different pieces,
Significant for us, and not very,
It can develop wisely, neatly,
Maybe - not very much: what, how - it is not clear.

Puzzles - crayons ... testing for strength,
They love perseverance, faith and accuracy.
Faith why? And then, that only with her
We will be able to report the whole picture.

In her relationship - we choose
Word and deed - we compose them into puzzles.
That day will come - the last word
Pull the puzzle - and the picture is ready!

What kind of "landscape" you will find in the end -
Your hands are a matter, and not at all God.
